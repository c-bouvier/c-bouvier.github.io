Clémence Bouvier

Since December 2023, I am doing a post-doc at the Ruhr Universität Bochum in the Symmetric Cryptography group.

Before that, I was a Ph.D student in the project-team COSMIQ of Inria Paris, under the supervision of Anne Canteaut and Léo Perrin, with a grant from Sorbonne Université.

I am mainly working on symmetric cryptography, and more precisely on arithmetization-oriented primitives.
